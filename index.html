Our Services

Custom Servicing

OGS can assist the clients in the formation and operation of a separate organization for this purpose with significant client control and with or without an equity interest for OGS. Alternatively this could be done under BOT (Build, Operate and transfer) Model as well

Channel Integration

Channel Integration Service with the state-of-the-art facility to perform any compliance related activities or for testing the device software/ hardware at the different payment channels and card read modes (EMV/ Magnetic Stripe/ Bar Code/ QR Code/ NFC)

Transaction Processing

SwitchOn integrates with point of sale solutions, data warehouses, credit application software, order management systems and custom business management solutions to ensure merchants within our closed-loop payment networks can authorize and settle customer transactions

Processing Interface

SwitchOn interface module will also establish the transaction processing capability by building the non ISO proprietary message structure/patterns too based on the other financial institutions format

Professional Services

Business study and analysis, Gap analysis, Risk and mitigation, Project management, implementation and deployment, testing/UAT, environment study , customizations and extensions, pre production, production and post production support

Certification and Testing

OGS offers its services in the areas of device certification (ATM / POS) and testing of transaction management systems and its applications. OGS well experienced team specialized in strategical planning and exhaustive testing knowledge who evaluates and certifies the system against the complete and customized functionality
